What is CFP Scanner?

CFP Scanner uses NLP techniques to extract information from Call for Papers, saving you the time of looking through long emails or finding relevant conferences manually. A Call for Paper can be contributed by sending it via email to cfpscanner@mail.com. Once contributed, any relevant details will be extracted and then the event will be seen on the homepage.
